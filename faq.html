Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our medical project

What is the purpose of this project?

Our medical project aims to improve healthcare accessibility and efficiency by providing tools for patient management, appointment scheduling, and medical data analysis.

How can patients register?

Patients can register through the registration form available on the homepage. Once registered, they can access their medical records and schedule appointments.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize data security. All patient information is stored securely and protected with encryption to ensure confidentiality.

Can doctors access patient records?

Authorized doctors and healthcare providers can access patient records to provide better treatment and ensure continuity of care.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via the Contact Us page. We’re available to assist with any issues or questions.
